What is Thrifting?

In terms, thrift means frugal, more precisely thrift is a behavior that is very concerned about how much money is spent to buy an item. So that it can be said, thrifting is a collection of items that are owned by someone and are no longer used or used. These items are then traded and can be reused by others. In other words, thrifting is the business of buying and selling used goods. In this thrifting business, it is not only used goods from well-known brands that can be traded. As long as the used goods are still functioning well and the quality is decent enough, they can be put into business. In addition, Thrifting is one of the right solutions for you when you are looking for goods with the desired price, size and model, even limited edition.
